WHY WE WRITE. . .

Robert Armstrong, Editor

I write because my truth and my perspective matters. Everyone's truth and perspective matter! However, we express it differently. I express my truth through writing. Writing isn't really a process to me or an arcane art, but it is a literal examination of the person I am on the inside. It is how I translate my soul. It is the strings by which I play the tune of my soul's harmony... or disharmony.
